# Lead Qualification Agent Demo Config

## Business
Business type: digital marketing agency.
Service: website development, SEO, paid ads, AI automation.

## Ideal lead
A good lead has:
- clear business need
- budget above 1000 EUR
- decision maker or direct contact
- timeline within 1–3 months
- business email or phone

## Bad lead
A weak lead has:
- no budget
- no clear need
- asks only for free consultation
- no contact details
- unrealistic deadline

## Qualification questions
Ask about:
- business type
- what problem they want to solve
- service needed
- budget range
- deadline
- contact details
- decision maker status

## Scoring rules
0–30: weak lead
31–60: medium lead
61–100: strong lead

## Escalation
Strong leads should be marked as hot and passed to sales.
Complaints or unclear cases should be reviewed by a human.
